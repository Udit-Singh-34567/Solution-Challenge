Why poverty?

Poverty is a complex and multifaceted phenomenon with various interconnected causes. It is important to note that the reasons for poverty can vary across different regions, countries, and communities. Some common factors contributing to poverty include:

  1. Lack of Education
  2. Unemployment
  3. Low Wages
  4. Health Issues

In our website, we are providing facilities to children who are unable to study due to a lack of resources. We also aim to raise funds to provide food to those in need.
